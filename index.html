🎯 Vision & Core Objective ▶

Transform IPPB Aarogya Savings Account from a wellness-oriented savings product into a healthcare-focused financial infrastructure. Enable employer-funded healthcare allowances, restrict spending to verified providers, integrate with ABDM-certified patient portals, link ABHA identities to healthcare financing, and track treatment adherence through transaction history.
